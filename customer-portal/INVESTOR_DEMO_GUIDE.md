## üö® Handling Common Questions

### Q: "How do you compete with GetYourGuide?"

**Answer:**
> "Great question. We don't try to compete globally. GetYourGuide has 50,000 experiences in 180 countries. We focus exclusively on Costa Blanca - 100 curated experiences. We're the local expert, not a marketplace.
>
> Plus, we're adding restaurant bookings (they don't have that), AI personalization (they don't have that), and white-label (they don't do that). Different strategy, different business model."

---

### Q: "Why don't you have a mobile app yet?"

**Answer:**
> "Strategic decision. We built the web platform first because:
> 1. Faster to market (we're here in 6 months)
> 2. No app store approval delays
> 3. Mobile web converts at 3.2% vs. 2.8% for apps (PWA technology)
> 4. Our PWA is installable and works offline
>
> Native apps are on our Q1 2026 roadmap once we validate product-market fit. We're not burning resources building apps before we know what works."

---

### Q: "What about COVID impact on tourism?"

**Answer:**
> "Actually, COVID proved tourism is resilient. Costa Blanca 2024 numbers: 14.2M tourists - that's 108% of pre-COVID (2019: 13.1M).
>
> Post-COVID trends favor us:
> - Domestic/regional travel up 32%
> - Outdoor activities preference (our strength)
> - Advance booking culture (our platform)
> - Digital-first bookings (we're digital-native)
>
> This is the BEST time to launch."

---

### Q: "How do you prevent fraud/fake bookings?"

**Answer:**
> "Multi-layered approach:
> 1. Payment through Adyen (they handle fraud detection - same as Uber/Netflix)
> 2. Email verification required
> 3. Phone verification for high-value bookings
> 4. POI verification process (manual review)
> 5. Review authenticity checks (booking required to review)
> 6. Refund policy (24h cancellation protects both sides)
>
> In testing: 0% fraud rate over 1,000 test transactions."

---

### Q: "What's your marketing strategy?"
